% title: Sample deck
% The deck bundled with the alix mobile app: three flip cards to try the
% review loop on. Replace it by opening your own decks (a later milestone).

# What does spaced repetition space out?
    reviews of a card
    ! Each successful review pushes the next one further into the future.

# Where does alix keep a deck?
    in a plain text file
    ! This deck is exactly such a file, bundled with the app.

# What grades can you give a card in alix?
    Fail, Partial, Pass
    ! Pass ends the card for this session; a miss brings it back later.
